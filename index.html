Crystal Collector

How To Play:


> You will be given a random target score at the beginning of the game.

> There are four different color crystals. By clicking on any of the four crystals you will add a random amount to your current score.

> The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click it and it will change each game.

> You win the game by matching your score to the target score. If you go over the target score you lose.

> The values of each crystal will change each time a new round starts.
